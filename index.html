Research Interests

My interests were on Pattern Recognition and Image Processing, especially on Fingerprint Recognition before 2016.


Now I'm interested in Machine Learning and Computer Vision. To be specific, my study focuses on clustering using deep neural networks, unsupervised transfer learning and unsupervised representation learning.


I'm a PC member for top AI coferences AAAI-20, IJCAI-20, and ECAI-20. I'm also a reviewer for to AI journals TNNLS, TCB (IEEE Trans. on Cybernetics), Neural Networks, and PLOS ONE.
